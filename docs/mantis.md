The MANTIS (Model-based Analysis of Threat Intelligence Sources)
Framework consists of several Django applications that, in combination,
support the management of cyber threat intelligence expressed in
standards such as:

- STIX
- CybOX
- OpenIOC
- IODEF (RFC 5070)
